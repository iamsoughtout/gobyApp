About us

Goby is the uber of public transport

We are a small team of passionate young Nigerians working to fix the problem of traffic gridlock in major cities in Nigeria(who knows! may be in Africa too). If you are tired of traffic in Lagos, so are we. We decided to find a solution to traffic problem because we are tired of complaining.

Too many personal cars on road at the same time is one major cause of traffic gridlock

Goby is an easy-to-use mobile app for people to book a clean, comfortable public transport ride to their choice destinations. Our solution revolves around working out a possible solution to reduce personal cars on the road, by creating a suitable and attractive alternative. Goby is the first Nigerian company to introduce Public Transport as a Service, PTAAS. We are hard at work creating a service that promises first class public transport system for the rich and poor
